  • how do I play...
    ...find the path to the finish star space, everytime you click a space that tile color will be added to your mixed bucket.
    ...the path will be as long as the level number (level 10 is 10 clicks away from the start position)
    ...you pass a level, if you mix the correct color and land on the target space.
  • mixing colors...
    ...each tile adds the same amount of color to the mixture, so the first click will be a 1:1 ratio, second 2:1, making the change more subtle as you add ;)
    ...you can use the swatch console to compare your current mixed color to the target.
    ...hover over your arrow options on the board to view/compare on your swatch console
  • finding a path...
    ...the current selected tile will display the amount of clicks you have left.
    ...you can not cross over your current path
    ...every incorrent try you will lose a life, but will gain lives when your complete a level.
    ...press the x on the current tile to try again.
